Privacy Policy

Khatu Shyam Ji Kirtan App

Last updated: April 2025

Aapki privacy humara priority hai. Hum aapka data kabhi bhi sell nahi karte aur na hi third-party advertisers ke saath share karte hain.

1. Information We Collect

Jab aap app mein apna profile banate hain, hum yeh information collect karte hain:

  • Name — aapke profile ke liye
  • Phone Number — account identify karne ke liye
  • Email Address — optional, agar aap dena chahein
  • Device ID — ek anonymous identifier aapke device ka

Hum automatically yeh bhi collect karte hain:

  • Karma Points activity — app usage (darshan, share, games etc.)
  • App settings — language preference, notification settings (locally stored)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Aapka Karma score track karne ke liye
  • App experience personalize karne ke liye
  • Aarti reminders aur Ekadashi notifications bhejne ke liye

Hum aapka data kisi bhi advertising, marketing, ya third-party service ke liye use nahi karte.

3. Data Storage

Aapka data Supabase (supabase.com) pe securely store hota hai, jo ek trusted cloud database provider hai. Data encrypted connection se transfer hota hai.

Kuch settings (jaise language, notification toggles) sirf aapke phone pe locally store hoti hain aur hamare servers pe nahi jaati.

4. Data Sharing

Hum aapka personal data kisi ke saath share nahi karte — na sell karte hain, na rent karte hain.

Sirf ek exception: agar law ya court order require kare, tab hum legally bound hain.

5. Notifications

App aapko yeh notifications bhej sakta hai:

  • Subah aur shaam aarti reminders (aap on/off kar sakte hain Settings mein)
  • Ekadashi alerts (automatically)

Aap kabhi bhi Settings mein jaake notifications band kar sakte hain.

6. Children's Privacy

Yeh app 13 saal se kam umra ke bacchon ke liye nahi hai. Hum jaante-bujhte 13 saal se kam ke users ka data collect nahi karte.

7. Account Deletion

Aap apna account aur saara data kabhi bhi delete kar sakte hain:

  • In-app: Settings → Delete Account
  • Online: Neeche diye link se request submit karein

Delete karne par aapka naam, phone number, aur saare Karma Points permanently remove ho jaayenge.

Request Account Deletion →

8. Changes to This Policy

Agar hum is policy mein koi bada change karte hain, to hum app update notes mein inform karenge. Latest version hamesha is page pe available rahegi.
